Sambosa Tech Company Handbook — Full Operations Manual (Revised Edition 2026)

Chapter 1: Company History and Mission Statement
Sambosa Tech was founded in 2018 with the goal of delivering secure, compliant enterprise infrastructure solutions to organizations worldwide. Over the past eight years we have grown to serve more than 2,400 customers across 18 countries. Our mission is to combine cutting-edge technology with the highest standards of governance, security, and operational reliability. Transparency, accountability, and customer-first decision making remain at the core of every process.

Chapter 2: Organizational Structure
The company is organized into five primary divisions — Engineering, Finance, Operations, Legal & Compliance, and People Operations. Each division is led by a Vice President who reports to the Chief Executive Officer. Regional headquarters are maintained in Dubai, Singapore, and London to ensure global coverage and regulatory alignment. All significant expenditures, vendor selections, and exception requests are reviewed by the Executive Governance Committee.

All employees are expected to uphold professional standards.
